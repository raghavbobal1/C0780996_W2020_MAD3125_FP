UTIL CRUNCHER

When you get your electricity, mobile and internet bill each month, you may face difficulties remembering all the bill details, so we an app to do that for you. We make calculating the cost of all your utilities fairly easy by combining all your necessary monthly bills in one place.

Some of Util Cruncher features include:

  • The ability to see the bills and their total

  • Adding a new user

  • A remember me switch so you don't have to enter your details everytime

  • Adding bills to the selected customer

  • Getting a detailed view of a bill with a click
